MINESWEEPER

Can you clear the minefield?


Instructions


Click on cells to reveal their contents, but be sure that you don't click on a mine


When a cell is cleared, it will display how many neighbouring mines it has.


If cells do not have neighbouring mines, they will uncover their neighbours until all uncovered cells are neighbored by mines.


You can keep track of mines by right-clicking on cells to mark them with flags!


Win by clearing the entire minefield, and avoiding all mines. Godd luck!
